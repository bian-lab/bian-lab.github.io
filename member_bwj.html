Dr. Wen-Jie Bian earned his bachelor’s degree in Biotechnology at Zhejiang University of Technology in 2008 and received his Ph.D. in Neurobiology at the Institute of Neuroscience, Chinese Academy of Sciences in 2016. From 2016 to 2022, Dr. Bian did postdoctoral training with Prof. Luis de Lecea at the Department of Psychiatry and Behavioral Science, Stanford University School of Medicine, and was promoted to an instructor in 2022. His work on neural circuit development and sleep function was published in journals including Cell, Nature Neuroscience, Sleep, Neuroscience Bulletin, and Developmental Neurobiology. Dr. Bian has received awards and honors including the Excellent Doctoral Dissertation of Chinese Academy of Sciences, Society for Neuroscience Trainee Professional Development Award, Human Frontier Science Program Long-term Postdoctoral Fellowship, Brain & Behavior Research Foundation Young Investigator Award, Sleep Research Society Outstanding Early Investigator Award. In 2023, Dr. Bian joined Westlake University as a Principal Investigator and started the Laboratory of Sleep and Neural Development.
