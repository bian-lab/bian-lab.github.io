Congratulations! The first publication of the BianLab.

2024-11-07, Xueqiang

We are excited to announce that the first publication of the Bian Lab has been published on the Frontiers in Neuroscience, which reviewed the effects of adolescent sleep disruption on depression and anxiety with focus on the assessment methodology and age. Congratulations to Ruiming and Wenjie! For more details, please check out the paper Adolescent sleep and its disruption in depression and anxiety.
